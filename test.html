Subjects in MHTCET

MHT CET 2021 syllabus includes four subjects - Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics/Biology out of which at "CrackIt!" we are going to primarily focus the following three subjects. The Exam for PCM group is subdivided into two phases : Physics+Chemistry in the first phase and Maths in the latter.

Physics

Physics has a weightage of 50 marks out of 200. Most of the questions are of the difficulty level 'Easy to Medium' and a few are over that. Combined Time allotted along with Chemistry is 90 minutes.

Chemistry

Chemistry is considered as one of the scoring subjects in MHT CET. Most of the questions are of the difficulty level 'Easy to Medium'. Combined Time allotted along with Physics is 90 minutes.

Maths

Out of the three subjects, Maths has the highest weightage of 100 out of 200 marks. It is a subject which plays the master role in determining your final MHT CET results. Time allotted is 90 minutes.
